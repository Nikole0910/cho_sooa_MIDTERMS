Why I think Dogs are better than Cats

Dogs are more trainable than cats.

Dogs are more loyal and protective towards their owners than cats.

Dogs are more social and can help their owners make friends, like running into someone else with a dog tends to make conversation.

Dogs can be great exercise partners and help their owners stay fit, since they have to be walked often.

Dogs can be trained to assist people with disabilities.

Dogs can provide emotional support and companionship to their owners.

Dogs can be trained to detect medical conditions like seizures and low blood sugar to assist their owners.

Dogs have a stronger sense of empathy and can read human emotions better than cats.

Why Cats are still good pets

Cats are easy to care for, quiet, and relatively affordable.

Cats understand personal space.

Cats are lower maintenance.

Cats cost less than dogs.

Cats are great companions and can reduce stress and anxiety.

Cats are more convenient than dogs for people who have less time to care for their pets.

Cats are quieter and cleaner compared to dogs, since they bark loudly.

Cats are great animals to have because most of them can stay indoors without throwing a fit, whereas you have to often walk dogs.
